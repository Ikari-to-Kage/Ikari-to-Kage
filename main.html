Stardust Stats ☆

☆  "And so, the stars guide you here..." ...or simply the fact that you don't carry dice in your pocket, and an idea for a character hit you. Welcome to the pile of dice I keep just for this occasion! With a little love from Lady Luck, and maybe a pinch or two of stardust, you'll have a quick reference for your character saved and ready for the next game!


   Below you will find all the 'first steps' in a table-top character's stats and creation; things that stretch across editions, systems, and worlds— be it AD&D, 3.5, Pathfinder, or even a homebrew system entirely seperate! So long as it uses the same, or similar, six basic stats with the usual roughly-20 point cap, this quick roll-and-save will work. Due to the vast number of differences in editions and systems, we can't give you everything— but we'll get you started, so the road ahead isn't quite so daunting.
